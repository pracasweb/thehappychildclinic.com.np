about our clinic

Welcome to "The Happy Child Clinic," where every smile matters! Beyond being a paediatric clinic, we're your dedicated partners in safeguarding your child's health and happiness.

At The Happy Child Clinic, our team of committed paediatricians and compassionate staff provides outstanding healthcare for children from infancy to adolescence. From routine check-ups and vaccinations to specialised treatments and developmental assessments, our comprehensive services are tailored to meet each child's unique needs.

We prioritise preventive care and empower families with knowledge and support to nurture healthy and joyful futures. Our clinic is thoughtfully designed to ensure your child's comfort, featuring inviting spaces and cutting-edge facilities for a positive healthcare experience.

Located conveniently in Biratnagar, Morang, we are committed to making high-quality paediatric care accessible and supportive for every family. Thank you for choosing The Happy Child Clinic as your trusted healthcare provider. Together, let's cultivate those precious smiles and contribute to your child's growth and well-being with compassion and expertise.

At The Happy Child Clinic, we believe in a patient-centered approach, focusing on your child's individual needs and well-being. From routine well-child check-ups to managing acute illnesses and providing guidance on developmental milestones, our team is here to support you every step of the way. We understand the importance of nurturing a trusting relationship with both you and your child, and we strive to create a warm and welcoming environment where families feel valued and cared for.

Whether it's immunizations, nutritional advice, behavioral health concerns, or any other pediatric need, our clinic is equipped to handle it all. We are also proud to offer telehealth services for your convenience, ensuring access to care from the comfort of your home.

Thank you for choosing The Happy Child Clinic for your child's healthcare needs. We look forward to partnering with you in promoting your child's health and happiness.
